What's Direct and Indirect Reported Speech?

Direct reported speech is when we say the words person said exactly as they said them.

Indirect reported speech is when we say the words person said without directly quoting them.

Type Direct Indirect
Actions I said "I'm going to play basketball." I said I was going to play basketball.
Requests/Commands I said to my friend "can you get me a coffee?" I asked my friend to get me a coffee.
Questions (Normal) I asked my mom "when's your birthday?" I asked my mom when her birthday is.
Questions (Yes/No) I asked my sister "are you home?" I asked my sister if she was home.

Round 1

Convert from direct to indirect speech

Ethan said, "I'm going to the store."

Ethan said __________________________

Ethan said, "I'm going to the store."

Ethan said he was going to the store.

"When are you going?" Tony asked Ethan.

Tony asked Ethan __________________________

"When are you going?" Tony asked Ethan.

Tony asked Ethan when he was going.

"Can you get me some milk?" Tony asked Ethan.

Tony asked Ethan __________________________

"Can you get me some milk?" Tony asked Ethan.

Tony asked Ethan to get Tony some milk.

Cristina said "________________________"
