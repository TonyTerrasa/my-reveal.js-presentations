What's Direct and Indirect Reported Speech?

Direct reported speech is when we say the words person said exactly as they said them.

Indirect reported speech is when we say the words person said without directly quoting them.

Type Direct Indirect
Actions I said "I'm going to play basketball." I said I was going to play basketball.
Requests/Commands I said to my friend "can you get me a coffee?" I asked my friend to get me a coffee.
Questions (Normal) I asked my mom "when's your birthday?" I asked my mom when her birthday is.
Questions (Yes/No) I asked my sister "are you home?" I asked my sister if she was home.

Round 1

Convert from direct to indirect speech

John said, "I will be late for the meeting."

John said __________________________

John said, "I will be late for the meeting."

John said he would be late for the meeting.

"I love you," said Sarah to Julia.

Sarah told Julia __________________________

"I love you," said Sarah to Julia.

Sarah told Julia she loved her.

"I won the race!" shouted Alex.

Alex shouted that __________________________

"I won the race!" shouted Alex.

Alex shouted that he won the race.

Emily said she wanted to learn English.

"________________________," said Emily.

Emily said she wanted to learn English.

"I want to learn English," said Emily.

Dennis asked me to help him with the problem.

Dennis asked "___________________________"

Dennis asked me to help him with the problem.

Dennis asked "can you help me with this problem?"

Maria asked where Jen was going.

"____________________" Maria asked Jen.

Maria asked where Jen was going.

"Where are you going?" Maria asked Jen.
